FAQ

Plans, limits, and how it works

What is the FREE plan limit?

FREE users can fetch and export up to 50 reviews per listing. Once the limit is reached, fetching stops automatically — even if the shop has more reviews. Upgrade to PRO to remove this cap and collect every review.

How do I scrape reviews?

Go to any Etsy listing page, open this extension, and click "Fetch Reviews". FREE accounts collect up to 50 reviews; PRO accounts have no limit.

Can I export reviews?

Yes. After fetching, export your collected reviews as CSV or XLSX. FREE exports include up to 50 reviews; PRO exports are unlimited.

What's in the PRO plan?

PRO removes the 50-review limit, so you can fetch and export every review on a listing. You also unlock Reviews by Month, Top Keywords, and priority support.

What happens when I hit 50 reviews on FREE?

Fetching stops at 50 reviews and you'll see a prompt to upgrade. Your collected reviews can still be exported. Upgrade to PRO anytime to continue past the limit on future fetches.

How do I cancel?

Cancel anytime from Settings. Your PRO access stays active until the end of the billing period, then you'll return to the FREE plan (50 reviews per fetch).

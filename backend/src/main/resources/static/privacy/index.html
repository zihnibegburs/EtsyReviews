Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Laodikeia ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the EtsyFetcher Chrome extension and website (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information we collect

Account information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information provided by Google (such as your name and profile picture) to create and manage your account.

Subscription information

If you subscribe to PRO, payment is processed by Paddle. We receive subscription status, plan type, and billing period information from Paddle. We do not store your full payment card details.

Usage data

We may collect basic technical logs (such as API request timestamps and error logs) to operate and improve the Service. Review data you fetch is processed locally in your browser unless you explicitly export it.

2. How we use information

  • Authenticate you and manage your account
  • Provide and maintain PRO subscription features
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve reliability and security of the Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth — for authentication
  • Paddle — for subscription billing and payment processing
  • Etsy — review data is read from publicly visible Etsy listing pages in your browser

These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data.

4. Data retention

We retain account and subscription data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request account deletion by contacting us.

5. Data security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption for API communication and secure token-based authentication. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at help@laodikeia.co to exercise these rights.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. The Chrome extension stores authentication tokens locally in your browser using Chrome's extension storage APIs.

8. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest revision.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Email help@laodikeia.co.

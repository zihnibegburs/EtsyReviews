Etsy Review Scraper for Etsy Sellers

EtsyFetcher is a Chrome extension that helps Etsy sellers collect, analyze, and export product reviews directly from Etsy listing pages.

What you can do

  • Fetch reviews from any Etsy listing page
  • Export reviews to CSV or XLSX
  • View reviews by month and top keywords (PRO)
  • Sign in with Google — no separate account needed

Plans

FREE — Up to 50 reviews per listing fetch.
PRO — Unlimited reviews, full export, and advanced analytics.

PRO starts at $8/month or $72/year (save 25%).

View pricing

How to get started

Install the EtsyFetcher Chrome extension, sign in with Google, and open any Etsy listing page. Click Fetch Reviews in the extension popup to begin.
